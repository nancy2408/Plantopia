Some common questions
were often asked

How can I control the system from my smartphone or computer?

The system can be controlled through a mobile application or a web-based dashboard that allows you to monitor and control the environmental conditions of your home, including the plants.

How effective is this in improving indoor air quality and overall health?

The potential to significantly improve indoor air quality and promote better health outcomes.

How many plants do I need in each room to maintain ideal oxygen levels and temperature?

Generally, one plant per 100 square feet of space is recommended, but this can vary depending on the specific needs of the room and the plants being used. It is important to monitor the oxygen levels and temperature regularly and adjust the number of plants as necessary.

Can this be customized to my specific needs?

Yes,it can be customized to meet specific needs. For example, the types of plants used can be selected based on personal preference or specific indoor air quality concerns. Additionally, the number and placement of sensors can be adjusted to target specific areas of concern. However, customization may require additional consultation with experts and potentially higher costs.
